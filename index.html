About Me

I am a PhD candidate in Economics at Lancaster University, with a focus in applied microeconomics. My research interests are in Health Economics, with additional interests in Development and Labor Economics. I am currently on the job market for the 2024/2025 academic year. My supervisors are Vincent O'Sullivan and Bruce Hollingsworth.
